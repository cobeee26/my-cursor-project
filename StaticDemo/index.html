Mathematics - Calculus Derivatives

Key concepts for derivatives:

  • Power rule: d/dx(x^n) = nx^(n-1)
  • Product rule: d/dx(fg) = f'g + fg'
  • Chain rule: d/dx(f(g(x))) = f'(g(x)) * g'(x)

Important examples:

  • d/dx(x^3) = 3x^2
  • d/dx(sin(x)) = cos(x)
  • d/dx(e^x) = e^x
